Who are we?

IKU and kuru are coed ultimate frisbee teams based in Tokyo, Japan. We play in tournaments both in Japan and around Asia.

Passionate about Ultimate? New to Ultimate?

Whether you are a seasoned veteran or have never thrown a disc, are a long-term resident or are just passing through Japan, we welcome you to join us at our bi-weekly pickup sessions. We look forward to introducing you to our brand of high-spirited ultimate.

Come play with us!
